## A. The "One Finger Per Fret" Rule

For this exercise, we're going to use a fundamental technique. We'll place our left-hand fingers on the first four frets.

* **Index Finger (1):** Is responsible for **Fret 1**.
* **Middle Finger (2):** Is responsible for **Fret 2**.
* **Ring Finger (3):** Is responsible for **Fret 3**.
* **Pinky Finger (4):** Is responsible for **Fret 4**.

Try to keep your fingers curved, and press the string down with the **very tip** of your finger, right behind the metal fret.
